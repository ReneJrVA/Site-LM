Meet Lethicia

Hello, my name is Lethicia Matos, I am 24 years, Brazilian, living in Belgium since 2019. Since my childhood i am passionate about photographing people and moments, when I was 17, I got my first camera and since then I have been transforming my vision in souvenirs. As a portrait photographer I am to bring satisfaction and joy to my clients, I like to show how my heart is involved in each click made by me. Everything done with love becomes something that brings satisfaction and my professional and personal goal in life is that this feeling is never lacking on my work. The câmera is my tool and through it I give a reason to everything that’s around me.
